Big Data

BigData is a term for data sets or raw data that are so large and complex that traditional data processing applications are inadequate to work. The challenges include analysis, capture, data curation, search, sharing, storage, transfer, visualisation, querying, updating and information privacy.The three V's of Bigdata are :- #volume #velocity #variety. You need optimal processing power, analytical capabilities and skills to extract meaningful value for Bigdata.
